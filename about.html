About Us

Our mission is simple: to demystify Korean food and make it accessible to everyone. We believe that food is a gateway to culture, and we want to help you experience the richness of Korean culture through its delicious and diverse cuisine. We're here to guide you, one bite at a time, so you can eat like a local and create unforgettable memories.

It all started with a simple observation: so many visitors to Korea were missing out on the full K-Food experience. They were eating amazing food, but they weren't always eating it the "right" wayâ€”the way locals do to maximize flavor and fun. From fumbling with Samgyeopsal wraps to neglecting the glorious sauces, we saw an opportunity to create a guide that was both informative and entertaining.

We are a passionate group of foodies, writers, and developers based in Seoul. We spend our days exploring the city's vibrant food scene and our nights documenting our adventures. We're not professional chefs, but we are professional eaters, and we're excited to share our passion with you.
